How it Works

AI-Powered Document Generation

Follow these steps to generate your legal rent agreement using our AI technology:

  1. Provide your basic information: Enter your name, address, and contact details.
  2. Input tenant details: Fill in the tenant's name, address, and contact information.
  3. Specify the property: Describe the rental property, including address and key features.
  4. Set terms and conditions: Define the rental period, payment terms, and any specific conditions.
  5. Review and confirm: AI reviews the input for completeness and legal compliance.
  6. Generate the document: AI compiles the information into a legally formatted rent agreement.
